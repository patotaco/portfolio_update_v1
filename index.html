History

Born in the Rockies, raised in the Rockies. Priorities growing up in the 90s: MTV, video games, sit-coms, football, friends, Disney, brands, labels and hip hop.

Moved to the Pacific Northwest after reading Dharma Bums, where I summons the spirt of Japhy Ryder and cultivate the PNW. With our Eurovan we escape the treacherous tether of roof, computer and mouse and embrace hotspots, trackpads and open container laws. Currently, aspiring to be freed by freelance whilst Jacky Kerouacing around with my graphically skilled wife and terrifically noisy children.
